Birds of Prospect Park
Brooklyn's Prospect Park is one of NYC's best birdwatching spots, attracting a menagerie of both birds and birders alike to take a rest in the shade of its trees and on the shore of Prospect Park Lake.
Friends You May Meet!
“I looked over at my neighbor, the song sparrow, and thought about how just a few years ago, I wouldn't have known its name, I might not have even known it was a sparrow, might not have even seen it at all. How lonely that world seemed in comparison to this one! But the sparrow and I were no longer strangers. It was no stretch of the imagination, nor even of science, to think that we were related. We were both from the same place (Earth), made of the same stuff. And most important, we were both alive.”
-Jenny Odell
